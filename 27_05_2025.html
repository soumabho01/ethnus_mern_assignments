FIFA World Cup 2014 News

The biggest surprise in the history of the FIFA World Cup qualifiers – and indeed in the history of international football – was recorded on 16 April 2001, when Australia beat American Samoa 31-0.
The legendary clash also brought stylish records for Archie Thompson, who netted 13 goals and set a new world record, which stands to this day, for the most individual goals in a single international match.
And though the defeat meant American Samoa became an inspiration for their subsequent recovery, they find it is now the subject of an acclaimed documentary, 'Next Goal Wins', charming across the world.
